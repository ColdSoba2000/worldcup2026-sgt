How to read the times. Every kickoff is converted to Singapore time (SGT, UTC+8), so a US afternoon/evening match shows up in the early hours or morning here — e.g. the opener kicks off Thu 11 Jun, 3:00pm ET / 1:00pm in Mexico City, which is Fri 12 Jun, 3:00am SGT, and the Final is Mon 20 Jul, 3:00am SGT. The small venue line shows the original local kickoff (ET / CT / MX / PT). Mexico venues don't observe daylight saving (UTC−6).

Note: knockout fixtures show the bracket slots (group winners/runners-up and best third-placed teams) until the group stage decides who fills them. Schedule per FIFA's published fixtures; kickoff times can shift slightly — confirm nearer the date.
